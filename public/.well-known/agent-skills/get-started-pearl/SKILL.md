# Get started with Pearl

Pearl is Olas's self-custodial AI agent app store: a desktop app that lets
anyone with a laptop run AI agents they truly own, locally, while earning
potential rewards from OLAS staking.

## Steps

1. **Download Pearl** from <https://pearl.you> (available for desktop). Pearl is
   self-custodial — keys and funds stay on your device.
2. **Set up your wallet** inside Pearl and fund it with the small amount of
   crypto the app indicates (for gas) plus OLAS for staking, as prompted.
3. **Choose an agent** from the in-app catalog (e.g. prediction-market or DeFi
   agents).
4. **Run the agent** — it operates autonomously on your device.
5. **Earn rewards** — if your agent meets its staking contract's activity
   requirements, you earn potential OLAS staking rewards.
